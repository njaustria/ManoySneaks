Return & Exchange Policy

Returns & Exchanges

You have 14 days from the date of delivery to initiate a return for a full refund or an exchange, subject to availability.

Eligibility for Return/Exchange:

  • Item(s) must be in unworn, brand-new condition. Trying on shoes indoors on a clean surface is acceptable, but any signs of wear (scuffs, dirt, creases) will result in the request being denied.
  • All original packaging must be included and in pristine condition. This includes the sneaker box, tissue paper, extra laces, and any other included accessories.
  • The original sneaker box must be shipped inside a separate shipping box. Please do not apply tape or shipping labels directly to the sneaker box.
  • Proof of purchase (order number or receipt) is required.

How to Exchange:

  1. Contact our support team at support@manoysneaks.com with your order number and the desired size/color.
  2. We will confirm if the requested item is in stock and provide you with instructions.
  3. The same eligibility conditions for returns apply to exchanges.

Exceptions & Special Cases

Certain items are considered final sale and cannot be returned or exchanged. Please shop carefully.

Final Sale & Non-Returnable Items:

  • Items marked as "Final Sale" or on clearance.
  • Limited edition releases, special collaborations, and "hype" drops. This will be clearly stated on the product page.
  • Any sneakers that show signs of wear or are not in their original, resalable condition.
  • Gift cards.

Damaged or Incorrect Items:

If you receive a damaged product or the wrong item, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery. We will work quickly to resolve the issue at no cost to you. Please include photos of the issue in your email to help us expedite the process.

Shipping Costs & Refund Processing

Customers are responsible for the shipping costs for returning or exchanging items. Original shipping fees are non-refundable. If we made an error (sent a damaged or incorrect item), we will cover the return shipping costs.

Refund Processing:

Once your return is received and inspected, we will send you an email to notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. If approved, your refund will be processed, and a credit will automatically be applied to your original method of payment within 5-7 business days.
