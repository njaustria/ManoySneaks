About Us

Your premier destination for authentic sneakers and exceptional service

Our Story

Welcome to ManoySneaks, your premier destination for the latest and greatest in athletic footwear! Founded in 2020, we've been serving sneaker enthusiasts with passion, authenticity, and unmatched customer service.

At ManoySneaks, we believe that the right pair of sneakers can transform not just your style, but your entire day. Whether you're hitting the gym, walking the streets, or making a fashion statement, we have the perfect kicks to match your vibe.

Meet Jeremy - Our Founder & Owner

Jeremy Manoy has been a sneaker enthusiast for over 15 years. What started as a personal passion for collecting rare and limited-edition kicks evolved into a dream to share that passion with the community.

With a background in fashion merchandising and years of experience in retail, Jeremy understands what customers want: quality products, fair prices, and genuine expertise. He personally curates every item in our collection, ensuring that only the best makes it to our shelves.

"I believe sneakers are more than just shoes - they're an expression of who you are. My goal is to help every customer find that perfect pair that makes them feel confident and authentic." - Jeremy Manoy
